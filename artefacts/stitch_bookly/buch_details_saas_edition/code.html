Synopsis

In a world where memories are traded like currency, a young archivist uncovers a series of encrypted journals that shouldn't exist. "Echoes of Infinity" explores the delicate intersection of technology and human soul, weaving a narrative that challenges the very definition of legacy.

Dr. Thorne’s latest masterpiece is a sprawling epic of speculative fiction, praised for its intricate world-building and the emotional resonance of its characters. A must-read for anyone fascinated by the future of human consciousness.
